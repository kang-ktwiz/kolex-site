Lee Ye-bin — The Choreography Everyone Copies

YB
KOLEX Editorial
June 7, 2026 · 5 min read

Born in 2001, Lee Ye-bin is one of KOLEX's youngest members — and arguably its most viral. With over 268,000 Instagram followers and a string of dance clips that regularly trend across Korean social media, she has quickly established herself as the face of a new generation of Korean sports entertainment.

The Choreography That Went Viral

What first caught the internet's attention was Ye-bin's 2025 out-song performance. The clip, shot in 4K from the stands, showcased a routine so precise and expressive that fans immediately began recreating it at home. Within days, the hashtag spread across TikTok and Instagram Reels, with fans from Japan, Taiwan, and Southeast Asia joining in.

The key to her viral appeal isn't just technical skill — it's her storytelling. Each routine feels like a miniature performance, with a beginning, climax, and emotional landing that stays with you long after the final beat.

Training and Dedication

Standing 168 cm and debuting with KOLEX at just 21 years old, Ye-bin has spoken candidly about the grueling preparation behind each season's routines. Pre-season training runs from January through March, with daily sessions that combine dance rehearsal, strength conditioning, and performance coaching. By opening day, each routine is polished to a level that would make professional dancers envious.

Connection with Fans

What distinguishes Ye-bin beyond her performance skills is her genuine relationship with fans. She regularly responds to comments, shares rehearsal bloopers, and posts morning greeting videos — small touches that make 268,000 followers feel like a close community rather than an audience.

Her fan base skews young and global. Japanese fans in particular have created dedicated translation accounts, making sure no update is missed by the international community.

The 2026 Season

The 2026 KBO season has already seen Ye-bin debut a new signature routine that's drawing comparisons to her 2025 breakout performance. With more international exposure planned through KOLEX's global digital strategy, this is a name you'll want to know.
