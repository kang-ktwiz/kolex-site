Lee Seo-yun — KOLEX's Youngest Star Is Already Impossible to Ignore

SY
KOLEX Editorial
May 31, 2026 · 4 min read

Born in 2006, Lee Seo-yun is the youngest member of KOLEX Sports Entertainment — and at 20 years old, she is already demonstrating the kind of instinctive talent that makes industry insiders take notice. Standing 168 cm with a natural stage presence that belies her age, Seo-yun has quickly become one of the most discussed newer members of the squad.

Youth as an Asset

There's a quality to Seo-yun's performances that veteran observers describe as "unguarded." Where more experienced performers have learned to manage their energy and expression across a long season, she performs with a freshness and total commitment that is genuinely infectious. Crowds respond to that quality — it reminds them why they came.

Multi-Sport Ambitions

Already performing across baseball, basketball, and volleyball at such a young age, Seo-yun has set herself up for a career trajectory that could eventually rival KOLEX's most established names. The foundation she's building now — across three sports, under the guidance of veterans like team captain Shin Se-hee — is exceptional.

Growing Fanbase

With 28K Instagram followers and growing, Seo-yun's fan community is in its early stages but already displays the depth of engagement that characterizes KOLEX's most passionate followings. As her career develops, this number will only climb.

For fans who want to follow the next generation of Korean sports entertainment from the very beginning, Lee Seo-yun is the name to watch right now.
