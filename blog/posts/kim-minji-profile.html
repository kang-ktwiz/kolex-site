Kim Min-ji — Tall, Expressive, and Just Getting Started

MJ
KOLEX Editorial
May 28, 2026 · 4 min read

At 173 cm, Kim Min-ji has a physical presence that commands attention before she's taken a single step. Born in 2005 and currently one of the younger members of KOLEX Sports Entertainment, she combines that natural advantage with a performance style that is expressive, energetic, and still rapidly evolving.

Physical Presence and What To Do With It

Height in sports entertainment is an asset, but only when paired with the technique and confidence to use it well. Min-ji has both. Her movements are expansive and deliberate — she understands how to fill space in a way that translates powerfully from the field level to the upper decks of large stadiums.

Multi-Sport Activity

Already performing across baseball, basketball, and volleyball at 21 years old, Kim Min-ji is one of KOLEX's most active multi-sport members. The breadth of that experience at such an early career stage is a significant advantage — she's developing the adaptability that the best long-term performers in the industry all share.

A Fanbase in Formation

With 15K Instagram followers, Min-ji's audience is in its formative stage. But the engagement rate within that community reflects something genuine — fans who are paying close attention and growing more invested as they watch her development unfold in real time.

In five years, people will say they followed Kim Min-ji from the beginning. Now is that beginning.
